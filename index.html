Fast Food VR Prototype

Prototype for a virtual reality fast food cooking game. Developed in Unity.

Features:
- Receive food orders that you must correctly serve.
- Grill meat on both sides without letting them burn.
- Make french fries and place them in different sized boxes.
- Use cups and a drink machine to prepare drinks.
- Put the whole order together without making any mistakes.
- Try to last as long as possible without losing.


3D Assets by Raúl Horta and Diogo Fernandes.
2D Assets and art by Diogo Fernandes.
Music and sounds by Pedro Henriques.

Circuit Puzzle creator for Unity

An asset for the Unity engine that allows users to create and customize a fully functional Circuit Puzzle and integrate it into their game with no coding required.

Features:
- Create a grid with any desired number of rows and columns.
- Switch the pieces on the grid to design the puzzle to your liking.
- Pick between 5 different piece types and rotate them freely.
- Use events to choose what happens when the puzzle is completed.
- Easily assign inputs to control the puzzle.
- A bunch of options to customize functionality.
- Use your own custom models for each piece type.

Cam Knight Prototype

A prototype for a sword fighting game using handmade computer vision. Developed in Unity.

- Use a real life sword to fight in-game.
- Use a menu to calibrate sword tracking.
- Parry attacks to stun enemies and attack them when they're down.
- Survive as long as you can against increasingly stronger enemies.

Knight model and animations by Raúl Horta.
2D Sprites and art by Diogo Fernandes.
